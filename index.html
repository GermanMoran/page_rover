About us



Our team is made up of students and professionals from different areas of knowledge in charge of developing the kuth-03 20 agriculture robot .The main objective of this project is to build a rover guided by GPS that allows sowing a corn seed taking into account different climatic variables. Working on this project allows us to exchange ideas and knowledge in technological areas and leadership.

Our Rover



The rover kuth-03 20 is a farming robot guided autonomous by GPS. It was designed, built and tested by team members. It is built by different electrical and mechanical modules that allow its best operation.

Time Line

Team formation 2020-6

Taking into account the announcement of IEEE colombia, we decided to form a team to participate in the challenge rover agriculture 2020. people interested in the subject were part of the group.

Exploratory Analysis 2020-7

Once the group is formed, we did a profound state of the art in order to identify possible solutions and ideas that will facilitate the construction of our rover. In this part, the specific tasks that the robot had to perform were defined. As well as some tasks were assigned to the team members to advance the project.

Implementation of functionalities: 2020-8

This stage is the longest, because we developed all the functional code in Arduino, which had to be implemented in each of the modules, in addition, all the parts of the rover were designed in the software Solid Work

Construction of the rover: 2020-11

Once all the code and the structural design were ready, we proceeded to assemble all the pieces and unify all the module codes into a single module. Different tests were carried out to verify a good functioning
